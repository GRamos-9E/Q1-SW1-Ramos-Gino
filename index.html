Lionel Messi: Greatest of All Time


Who is Lionel Messi ? Leo was born in the small town of Rosario, Argentina on June 24, 1987. He is known to be one of if not the greatest footballers to ever walk this planet. He is one of many things: footballer, teammate, father, friend, and the love of my life. During his childhood he showed signs of greatness and had a lot of support from his abuela. After a few years, a lot of tragedies happen in his life such as his abeula dying and him getting diagnosed with a rare syndrome that reduces the amount of hormones needed to grow. Because of these tragedies are why his celebration is him pointing to the sky after every goal (for abeula) and his short stature standing only at 5'7 feet.

Around the summer of 2005, scouts of FC Barcelona discovered the Argentinian wonderboy and rushed so much to sign him his contract was handed in a napkin. He was then in the Barcelona Under 16 roster where he was trained in the legendary training facility known as La Masia. During this time Lionel strived and did well yet felt homesick due to Argentina being so far away. A few years later in 2008, that newly appointed manager Pep Guardiola started his stint as head manager. Around this time, he saw the potential in Messi and played him more. The addition of Messi boosted the already strong and deadly team with the likes of Xavi Hernandes, Andres Iniesta, and Carles Puyol in the squad. Just a year after Guardiola's appointment, FC Barcelona won the Uefa Champions League, one of the most prestigious club competitions in the world with Messi also winning the Balon D'or in 2009 for his impressive performance.

A few years later in 2012, my love produced one of the most statically impressive seasons ever in the history of the sport. He finished the year with a record of 91 goals in a calendar year which still has not been broken until today. He dominated the Spanish league and won that and the domestic cup (Copa Del Rey). Lionel Messi was now one of the most feared players in Europe as he reached the height of his career. A few years later, 2 assets made by Barcelona will prove to be one of the best in Uruguayan striker, Luis Suarez from Liverpool and Brazilian wonder Neymar Jr. from Santos F.C they have made as just a few years later in 2015 as they won the treble winning the Spanish league, Copa Del Rey, and Uefa Champions League under the trio known as MSN.

After the perfect year of 2015 was when things started to go downhill. During the year of 2017, Neymar Jr. was sold to French club: Paris Saint-Germain for a world record €222 million which translates into PhP13,904,652,715.94. Things went even more downhill as they got knocked out of the Champions League in shocking fashion as they blew their goal advantage to A.S Roma. Another heartbreak occured in 2019 even after Lionel Messi scored a spectacular hattrick in the first game winning it 3-0, Liverpool came back in the second game and won 4-0 completing one of the most spectacular comebacks in football. One of the issues that have been happening behind the scenes was Barcelona's debt. This was due to the money wasted on Messi's contract, expensive players that did not live up to their price, and a lot of loans. That was why during the year of 2021 after around 20 years with F.C Barcelona, Lionel Messi left his childhood club and joined Paris Saint-Germain on a free.

During the summer of 2021, Lionel Messi was welcomed into Paris with excitement and hype as he teamed up with up and coming star Kylian Mbappe and his former teammate, Neymar Jr. PSG were hyped as the best team in the world and then dissapointed as they lost in the first round to Real Madrid; blowing a 2-0 goal advantage as Karim Benzema silenced everyone by knocking out this "superteam". Messi's time in PSG was marred with drama such as locker room disputes, management issues and fans requesting for his removal from the club. After all of that drama the World Cup happened and the rest is history.
